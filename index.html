About

I always feel the world is most honest in silence. In that quiet, textures step forward, the smallest lights begin to matter, and people reveal something true. My practice is not about showing the brightest moments — it is about listening to the dark and recording what answers back.

Behind the Project

Why do you shoot with a phone?

It’s simple.
Fast.
Uncomplicated.
And it lets me stay present.

What's my process?

I start by collecting ideas, letting them sit, and seeing which ones stick.
Then I build, tweak, and polish until everything feels natural.
I do things step by step, no pressure, no drama—just steady progress.
